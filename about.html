About

I am an illustrator, designer, and developer with a passion for learning. I love stories about real human experiences and strive to represent people of all backgrounds.

Through art, I have found a form of self expression that helped me when I felt lost. Art is an inherently human quality that brings a necessary enjoyment and beauty to life. Without art, where would we be?

When I am not drawing, I can be found weightlifting, playing games, cooking, playing music, learning a language, or otherwise creating in some capacity.
