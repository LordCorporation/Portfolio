Strikethrough (Summer 2023)

An author who has grown to hate their own creations and gone into their work to destroy the characters they made.

I worked with a programmer and a sound designer on this game. This jam was set up by Game Maker's Toolkit.

  • Play Strikethrough

Characters

I designed these characters based on tropes: the dame, the detective, the child, and the henchman.


Environment

I created the environment in specific sections and based the decorations off that: the crime scene, the park, the office, and the alley. The map was designed like a page in a comic book and each of the locations were in one section of the map.
