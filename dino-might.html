Dino-Might

Dino-Might is a couch co-op fighting game where you control a tiny-armed T-Rex in a prehistoric smackdown! Customize your look, pounce on your opponents, dodge pitfalls, and watch out for the incoming meteor shower in a wild battle for survival. Who will be the last dino standing?
