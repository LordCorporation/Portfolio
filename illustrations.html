PARADOX

PARADOX is a tabletop RPG system I worked on with my partner. Mothership, published by Tuesday Knight Games, is a huge inspiration to PARADOX, based around movement in 3D space. The illustrations I completed are for the cover, conflict section, characters section, and ship creation section respectively.
